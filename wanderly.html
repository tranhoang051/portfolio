Wanderly Travel is a smart and intuitive travel app that helps users organize trips, discover hidden gems, and keep track of expenses, all in one place.

Section 01

Problem

Travelers often feel overwhelmed planning trips, especially when trying to discover authentic experiences while staying within budget.

Section 02

Objective

Wanderly Travel helps travelers plan, organize, and enjoy trips effortlessly. The final prototype demonstrates these goals through a clean, user-friendly interface.
