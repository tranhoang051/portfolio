About Me

Tran Hoang — UI/UX Designer

During my design journey, I have had the opportunity to engage in hands-on projects that combine technology and creativity. I focus heavily on designing user-friendly interfaces and exploring the principles of accessibility and user-centered design. These experiences have provided me with practical insights and highlighted the importance of creating technology that serves diverse needs effectively.

What excites me most about UI/UX is the chance to blend empathy, problem-solving, and creativity. For me, design isn't just about how something looks, it's about how it works and how it makes people feel.

Outside of design, I enjoy making matcha and photography. Both give me space to slow down, observe details, and find inspiration, the same qualities I bring into my design process.
